— Our Story

Rooted in Craft, Driven by Flavour

Brewed & Bound was born in a small garage in Bangalore in 2018, when founder Arjun Mehta returned from a trip to Coorg carrying 5 kgs of the most extraordinary beans he'd ever tasted.

Today we work directly with 12 family farms across India's coffee belt — paying fair prices, supporting sustainable practices, and sharing the stories of the people behind your cup.

Every batch is small-roasted, quality-tested, and shipped within 48 hours. Because freshness isn't a luxury, it's the point.
